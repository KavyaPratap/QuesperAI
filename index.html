Built By A Teacher's Family

"We watched teachers spend their evenings formatting papers instead of spending time with family."

Hi, I'm Kavya Pratap Singh (KavyaPratap, Kavya), a third-year Computer Science Engineering student specializing in AI and Machine Learning.

The idea behind Quesper didn't start in a computer lab. It started during the COVID-19 lockdown when I was in Class 10. My father, a high school mathematics teacher, would spend hours every evening formatting exams, matrix brackets, and algebra symbols. It was unpaid clerical work that stole hours away from our family.

Determined to fix this, I began building algorithms to compile math structures automatically. Once I learned Android and systems architecture, Quesper was born.

Our Mission: Teaching is a noble profession, not a typesetting job. Quesper returns those hours back to teachers so they can spend them with their loved ones.

What is Quesper AI?

+

Quesper AI is an advanced, AI-powered question paper generator designed specifically for school teachers, tutors, and educational institutions. It utilizes Google Cloud Vertex AI and Gemini models to convert scanned printed textbooks (including NCERT, Exemplar, etc.), worksheets, and handwritten notes into high-fidelity editable MS Word documents (.docx) and printable vector PDFs, saving educators hours of typing and layout formatting.

How does the AI Question Paper Generator work?

+

Our question paper creator app operates in four simple steps:

  • Upload or Scan: Capture a photo of any exam book or NCERT textbook page using the Android app.
  • AI Extraction: The built-in Math OCR engine detects and parses mathematical equations, algebra matrices, calculus symbols, and geometry texts with 99.8% LaTeX accuracy.
  • Compile & Customize: Select chapter weights, difficulty levels, and total marks using our blueprint builder.
  • Export: Instantly download the formatted paper in Microsoft Word or PDF formats.

Can it handle complex mathematical equations and LaTeX structures?

+

Yes. Quesper AI is engineered as a state-of-the-art maths question paper making tool. It incorporates a local offline math typesetting compiler (LatexToOmmlConverter) and cloud engines to translate complex math formulas (fractions, integrals, summation, square roots, matrix grids) into native Office Math XML for Microsoft Word. You can edit every equation natively inside MS Word.

Does Quesper AI support CBSE, NCERT, and State Board syllabi?

+

Absolutely. The app features a pre-indexed School Library covering Class 6 to Class 12 mathematics, physics, and chemistry. Teachers can browse CBSE NCERT syllabus questions, select them by topic or difficulty (Easy, Medium, Hard), and build test papers without manually typing a single word.

Can I import handwritten questions and test papers?

+

Yes, our OCR digitizer can capture handwriting. Tutors can scan handwritten sheets, and Quesper's Vertex AI model extracts and sanitizes the equations, turning them into clean digital formats.

Is there a WhatsApp Assistant for generating exams?

+

Yes, our Pro and Value plans include a smart WhatsApp Assistant. Tutors and school teachers can interact with the Quesper bot directly on WhatsApp to retrieve question blueprints, syllabus weightages, and export generated papers.

What file formats does the test creator support for export?

+

Quesper supports double export formats: high-fidelity, print-ready vector PDF files and fully editable Microsoft Word (.docx) documents with native math formats.
